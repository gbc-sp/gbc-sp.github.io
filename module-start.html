Smarter Learning

Learning is the change in one's behaviour or knowledge which comes about as a result of experience.

We all learn in different ways, and sometimes individuals have different methods of learning depending on the job they need to do.

The Skillpod Smarter Learning skill is based on understanding yourself as a learner so that you can start to learn faster and design learning in a way that best suits your personal needs. This begins with a self-assessment, the Personal Learning Preferences (PLP) which combines variables such as you Multiple intelligences (MI) strengths with your personal interests and career interests. It helps you learn from past successes and create a process that works best for you.
